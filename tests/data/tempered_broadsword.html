The Galaxy Hammer is a club weapon that can be purchased for 75,000g at the Adventurer's Guild after obtaining a Galaxy Sword.

It can be combined with Galaxy Soul (3) and Cinder Shard (60) in the Forge to create the Infinity Gavel.

History

  • 1.1: Introduced.
  • 1.4: Description changed from "It's made from an ultra-light material you've seen before."
  • 1.5: Can now be changed into an Infinity Gavel. Level increased from 9 to 12, sell price increased from 450g to 600g.
